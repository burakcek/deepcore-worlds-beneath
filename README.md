
# DeepCore: Worlds Beneath

**DeepCore: Worlds Beneath** is an original, single-player voxel survival-exploration game prototype. The player descends through a hostile planet using a heat-limited Plasma Drill, builds a protected underground home, survives roaming creatures, and restores a lost machine civilization's reactor.

It uses original names, mechanics, visual design, and code; it is not a Minecraft clone and does not include Minecraft assets or branding.

## Game loop

1. Mine Ferrite, Crystal Cores, and Coolant.
2. Descend through five increasingly hostile zones.
3. Use the drill to defend yourself from creatures and robots.
4. Build steel walls with **B** and create a permanent safe base with **H**.
5. Reach the Ancient Machine City at approximately 560 m.
6. Deliver 18 Ferrite and 8 Crystal Cores to restore the Ancient Reactor.

Death returns the player to the Surface Outpost or their Home Core, with a small credit recovery cost. Progress and inventory are saved in the browser automatically.

## Controls

- **A/D** or **arrow keys** — move
- **W**, **up arrow**, or **Space** — jump
- **Hold left click** — mine blocks or attack enemies
- **Hold B + click** — place a steel wall (cost: 1 Ferrite)
- **H** — deploy a Home Core (cost: 8 Ferrite + 2 Crystal Cores)

The Home Core heals the player and forces creatures away within its blue protective field. A player can deploy one core per expedition.

## Zones

| Zone | What changes |
|---|---|
| Surface Outpost | Safe starting area and early resources |
| Crystal Caverns | Crystal Golems and richer veins |
| Frozen Tunnels | Frost Stalkers and coolant deposits |
| Magma Chambers | Lava Worms and dangerous high-value deposits |
| Ancient Machine City | Guardian Drones and the final reactor |

## itch.io page copy (English)

### Short description

Drill into a living planet, build a safe underground home, survive hostile creatures, and restart an ancient reactor in this original voxel survival adventure.

### Full description

**DeepCore: Worlds Beneath** is a sci-fi mining and survival game about going where no expedition has returned from. Armed with a Plasma Drill, you descend into procedurally generated strata to collect rare resources, fight alien threats, and uncover the remnants of a forgotten machine civilization.

Every descent asks a question: push deeper for a rare Crystal Core, or return to the safety of your base? Build steel walls, deploy a Home Core that repels creatures and repairs your suit, then plan the journey through Crystal Caverns, Frozen Tunnels, Magma Chambers, and the Ancient Machine City. Restore the reactor and complete the expedition.

**Features**

- Five underground zones with escalating enemies and resources
- Heat-limited drilling, mining, combat, and exploration
- Buildable walls and a deployable healing safe zone
- Death, recovery, persistent browser saving, and a final campaign objective
- Original sci-fi voxel world with no third-party game assets
